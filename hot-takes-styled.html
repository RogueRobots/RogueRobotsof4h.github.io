Rogue's Hot Takes 2023

We have the hottest takes this year.

Superpower: Flying or Invisibility

Being invisible is better than flying because if you're invisible you can still fly in a plane. But if you can fly, there's nothing that you can do to become invisible.

Seasons: Spring, Summer, Fall, or Winter

Winter is clearly the best season. You can always get warmer in the winter, but it's hard to get cooler in the summer. Plus snow is awesome. And my birthday is in the winter!

Snacks: Potato Chips or Cookies

Potato chips are smaller than cookies, so you can eat more of them in a single sitting. Also, they are good when added to other foods like sandwiches and hot dogs. But potato chips taste good also when added to sweet foods like Reece's peanutbutter cups. Adding cookies to savory foods doesn't work as well.

Scenes: Mountains or Ocean

Mountains are so cool. Some of them come from volcanic activity, like Mt Ascutney, one of my neighborhood mountains. They are fun to climb. At the summit you can see forever. And you are less likely to drown on a mountain.
