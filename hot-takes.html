Hot Takes

1. Create a Title for Your Page

Create a new HTML page called hot-takes.html. Add a header for your page.

2. Add in Your Takes

Choose between each alternative and explain your choice with one or two sentences. Create a subsection on your page for each take.

Remember that everything you post on the internet can be seen by strangers. Do not post anything that you would not tell or show to a stranger.

Takes

  • Superpower. Flying or invisibility
  • Seasons. Winter, spring, summer, or fall
  • Snacks. Potato chips or cookies
  • Scenes. Ocean or mountains

3. Add Images

Search on Flickr for images with Creative Commons liscense illustrate each of your choices. Add them to your page. Use the <figure>, <img>, and <figcaption> HTML elements to structure your images. Remember to include a description of each image using the alt attribute.

4. Use CSS to Style Your Page

You may style the background, text, and images however you like.

Create a stylesheet called style.css and link it to your page by adding a <link> element to the <head> of your page like this: 

       <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">

Add your CSS style rules in style.css.

5. Upload Your Page to GitHub

Remember to save your work and publish it on the internet!
